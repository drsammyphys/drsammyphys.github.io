Volume 3: Thermodynamics

This volume is a self-contained introduction to the thermal interaction between macroscopic systems due to a difference in their temperatures. A central concept in this field is the idea of thermodynamic equilibrium. Chapters 1-4 are devoted to the study of properties of systems in a thermodynamic equilibrium. Kinetic theory provides a microscopic explanation of some parts of thermodynamics. A brief introduction of kinetic theory is provided in the last chapter.
